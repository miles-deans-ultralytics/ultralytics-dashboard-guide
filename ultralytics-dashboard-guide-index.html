The 4 Key Metrics

At the top of the dashboard you'll always see four headline numbers. Think of them as your scoreboard â€” they summarise all site traffic for the selected date range.

Active Users

People who were actively browsing the site during the selected period â€” clicked, scrolled, or interacted. The most direct measure of real engagement.

The number of visits. One person can have multiple sessions â€” visit Monday and again Friday and that's 1 user but 2 sessions.

Understanding % Changes

Below each metric you'll see two percentage changes. A green arrow means up, red means down.

YoY Year over Year

Compares this period to the same period last year. A +200% YoY means you had triple the traffic compared to this time last year.

PoP Period over Period

Compares this period to the previous period of the same length. Looking at 30 days? It compares to the 30 days before that.

Filters

The dropdown bar at the very top of the dashboard is your control panel. Every time you change a filter, all charts and numbers update instantly to match your selection.

Filter What it does
Source Channel Filter by where visitors came from.
